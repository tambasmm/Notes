
### **1. What is Jenkins?**

Jenkins is an open-source automation server used to build, test, and deploy software. It helps developers automate parts of the software development process, especially continuous integration (CI) and continuous delivery (CD). Jenkins supports a wide range of plugins that integrate with many tools in the DevOps ecosystem.

***

### **2. History and Background**

*   **Origin**: Jenkins was originally developed as *Hudson* in 2004 by Kohsuke Kawaguchi at Sun Microsystems.
*   **Evolution**: After Oracle acquired Sun, a dispute led to the community forking Hudson into Jenkins in 2011.
*   **Growth**: Jenkins quickly became the most popular CI/CD tool due to its flexibility, plugin ecosystem, and strong community support.
*   **Current Status**: Jenkins is maintained by the Jenkins community and governed by the Continuous Delivery Foundation (CDF).
